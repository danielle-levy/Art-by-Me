Store name: Art by Me

What are you selling?

  • My abstract & minimal drawings painted on Procreate app via iPad & apple Pencil
  • My art with a pallet of your liking (Pick Color feature)
  • A personal drawing of a photo you send me (Image to Art feature)
  • T shirts with my textual designs

What additional page(s) did you add? How to operate it?

  1. /HomePage.html - static page showcasing some drawings and a banner leading to the store
  2. /AboutUs.html - static page that describes who are we; me and my partner in this project
  3. /User.html - a personal page that has information about the user that is logged in such as it's username, log activity and purchases. You can view it by clicking on the user symbol on the top left of the website which says 'hello, username'
  4. /Purchases.html - a personal page that has detailed information regarding the user's purchases (logged in). You can view it by getting into your user page and then click the link to view tour purchases.
  5. /ContactUs.html - a form that sends a message to the system from the user
  6. /Messages.html - an additional page for the admin that displays the messages that were sent via the contact me form. The admin only can view it by going to the user page and then click 'View Messages Page'.
  7. /PickColor.html - a feature that allows costumers to choose a color background of their liking to various artworks
  8. /ImageToArt.html - a feature that allows costumers to upload their images to purchase a personal drawing - which is the perfect way to cherish beautiful moments! (and the perfect gift). You get there by getting into the store page (/Store.html) and then clicking on the banner which says: "MAKE YOUR MEMORIES INTO ART". In the page you will find instructions on how to upload your desired photos: just by clicking the button which says "CLICK TO SELECT IMAGES", once you choose - a preview of your chosen photos will pop and once you are pleased with your choices you can click SUBMIT and it will be added to your cart.
